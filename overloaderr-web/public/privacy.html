PRIVACY POLICY

1. Information Collection

The Overloaderr stores the vast majority of your data locally on your device using SwiftData and integrates securely with Apple Health (HealthKit). We prioritize your privacy and do not transmit your personal identifying information to external servers without your explicit consent.

2. HealthKit Integration

We request access to Apple Health (HealthKit) to read and write workout data and analyze your fitness activity. This data is processed locally on your device to build your fitness profile. We do not sell or share your HealthKit data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your user profile, logs, and workout preset data are stored securely on your iCloud account and synchronised across your devices using Apple's native technologies. We do not maintain an external database of user accounts.

4. Analytics and Usage

We may collect anonymous crash reports and usage analytics to help improve the app's stability and features. This data contains no personally identifiable information.

5. Terms of Use (EULA)

By using The Overloaderr, you agree to the standard Apple Terms of Use (EULA).
